# 🌿 KeenKeeper

> Never lose touch with the people who matter most.

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship manager that helps you stay intentional about your friendships — track contacts, log interactions, and spot who needs your attention.

---

## 🛠 Built With

`React` · `Vite` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Recharts` · `React Router`

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Friend Tracking** — See who's On-Track, Almost Due, or Overdue at a glance
- **Quick Check-in** — Log a Call, Text, or Video with one tap and watch it appear on your Timeline instantly
- **Interaction Analytics** — A live donut chart breaks down your interactions by type; hover to see counts
